About

VEDA VFX is a team of professional artists whose expertise lies in Visual Effects and Motion Graphics for films, commercials and television. Located in Lucknow, our creative and technical services cater to the Entertainment and Media industries. VEDA VFX Thinking Cell is a fully self-contained production suite drawing in skilled artists, cutting-edge production techniques and expert use of VFX CGI and Motion Graphics to great effect. The team are on hand to help you realise the true potential of your creative visions.
