Services

End-to-end picture post & virtual production

Rotoscoping

We provide high-quality rotoscoping services with precision and attention to detail. From complex character outlines to intricate motion tracking, our expert artists ensure clean mattes that integrate seamlessly into any visual environment.

Paint & Prep

Paint & Prep Our Prep team specializes in removing rigs, wires, tracking markers, and unwanted elements with flawless results. We restore frames to their original quality, ensuring smooth, distraction-free visuals for final compositing.

Compositing

Compositing At the heart of VEDA VFX, compositing brings all elements together into a single stunning frame. Our skilled compositors merge live-action footage, CGI, matte paintings, and effects with seamless color correction, keying, and integration—creating visuals that feel real and immersive.

Matchmove

Our Matchmove and Rotomation services provide the perfect foundation for integrating CG with live-action. From object and camera tracking to recreating realistic movements, our team ensures accurate geometry alignment and motion replication— bringing digital assets to life in sync with the real world.

CG & FX

We deliver top-notch CG & FX services with creativity and precision. From realistic 3D modeling and animation to immersive visual effects, our skilled artists craft stunning visuals that elevate any project. With cutting-edge tools and a passion for detail, we ensure seamless integration and captivating results for films, ads, and digital media.
